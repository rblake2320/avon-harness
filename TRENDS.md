# Market Trends — What the Product Must Account For
*Last updated: June 24, 2026. Re-verify quarterly — this market moves fast.*

This document records current market reality so product decisions are grounded in what's
actually happening. Each trend has a **product implication**. Sources are dated; re-check
anything older than 2025. Shared market lineage with MK Copilot's TRENDS.md.

---

## 1. The authenticity backlash against AI content — THE defining constraint

**The trend:** Consumer preference for AI-generated creator content collapsed — only **26%**
prefer AI creator content in 2026, down from 60% in 2023 (Digiday, Jan 14 2026). Aerie's
"no AI… ever" pledge became its most-liked Instagram post ever, tracking +23% Q4 sales
(Marketing Brew, Mar 30 2026). Platforms now **label and penalize** AI content:
- Instagram "AI Creator" labels (May 4, 2026)
- TikTok auto-labels AI content
- YouTube mandatory AI disclosure (enforced May 2025) — undisclosed AI **can lose ad revenue**

**Product implication (critical):** Our social/content skill must make Avon reps *faster and
more authentic*, never produce detectable "AI slop":
- Generate **drafts and talking points the rep personalizes**, not finished posts to paste.
- Favor raw, personal, phone-shot framing. Output should read like the rep wrote it.
- Never auto-post. Rep-in-the-loop always.
- Coach authenticity in-app — turn the platform risk into our selling point: compliant AND real.

This is the most important trend for a rep AI tool in 2026. Build around it.

## 2. FTC is now prosecuting INDIVIDUAL reps for income claims

**The trend:** Enforcement shifted to individuals — **Stormy Wellington** (Apr 2026),
**IM Mastery Academy $795.8M judgment** (May 2026), all for unsubstantiated income/lifestyle
claims. r/antiMLM holds at ~853K members.

**Product implication:** Direct validation of our 58-pattern income-claim filter. It matters
*more* for Avon because **Avon publishes no US Income Disclosure Statement** — every income
claim a rep makes is unsubstantiated by definition. The filter is the rep's liability shield.
Never weaken it. Surface *why* a phrase was blocked so reps learn the rule.

## 3. Live selling is where the growth is

**The trend:** Livestream shopping is ~22% of US TikTok beauty sales; live sellers saw
**+84% sales YoY**; live converts at **5–12%** vs 2–4% standard (McKinsey State of Beauty
2026; beautymatter.com Mar 2026). Beauty is the #1 TikTok Shop category.

**Product implication:** A **live-selling assistant** is high-value for Avon — real-time
Anew/Skin So Soft talking points, objection handling, on-the-fly compliance, and the
guest-checkout reminder live. Maps to where reps actually grow revenue. (ROADMAP 1.2.0+.)

## 4. Platform rules + Avon's own-channel model

**The trend:** TikTok's US divestiture closed Jan 22, 2026 (ban averted). **Avon channels
sales through its own e-store**, not third-party marketplaces. Reps build organic social
presence and drive customers to their personal Avon online store.

**Product implication:** This dovetails with Avon's #1 revenue leak — **guest checkout**.
Our content and follow-up skills should drive customers to **create an account through the
rep's link** (not guest checkout, not a third-party marketplace). Organic-to-official-store
motion is exactly what earns the rep commission. Encode it so we never coach a move that
loses the rep money or risks their account.

---

## 5. Gen Z avoids classic MLM but responds to authentic UGC

**The trend:** Younger people avoid traditional downline-recruiting MLM (18–24 = 6.9% of
direct sellers; 55+ = 28%, WFDSA STATS 2024). Winners use authentic user-generated content
and hybrid affiliate-style links, not recruitment pitches.

**Product implication:** Skills bias toward **product storytelling and customer
relationships** (Anew results, Bug Guard seasonal use, Skin So Soft), not recruitment
scripts. De-emphasizing recruitment content also reduces FTC exposure.

## Net: the 2026 thesis for this product

A winning Avon rep AI tool in 2026 is **a compliance-safe authenticity amplifier** that
drives customers to the rep's official Avon store (not guest checkout, not marketplaces),
keeps reps clear of FTC and platform penalties, and points them at live selling and organic
motion — where revenue is actually growing. Everything on the roadmap ladders up to that.
